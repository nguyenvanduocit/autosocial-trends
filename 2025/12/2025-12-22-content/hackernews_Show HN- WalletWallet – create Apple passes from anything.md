# WalletWallet

A simple utility to convert physical barcodes into digital passes for Apple Wallet®. Entirely free and runs directly from your browser.

1
Enter your membership or loyalty card barcode data.

2
Configure the appearance and titles for your pass.

3
Download and open the file to add it to your Wallet.
