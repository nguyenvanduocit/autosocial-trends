Waymo said Saturday that it was stopping service across San Francisco after numerous online videos showed its autonomous vehicles snarling traffic during the [citywide blackout](https://missionlocal.org/2025/12/sf-power-outage-pge-electricity/).

‚ÄúWe have temporarily suspended our ride-hailing services given the broad power outage in San Francisco,‚Äù wrote Suzanne Philion, a company spokesperson, a little after 7 p.m. ‚ÄúWe are focused on keeping our riders safe and ensuring emergency personnel have the clear access they need to do their work.‚Äù

About a [third of San Francisco](https://pgealerts.alerts.pge.com/outage-tools/outage-map/) lost electricity throughout the day Saturday after rolling Pacific Gas & Electric Co. blackouts hit some 125,000 homes and businesses. PG&E [said](https://x.com/PGE4Me/status/2002597756448198853) at 8:30 p.m. that power should start to be restored ‚Äúlater this evening.‚Äù
