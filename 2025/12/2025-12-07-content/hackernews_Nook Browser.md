## Frequently asked questions

What is Nook?

Nook is a fast, modern WebKit browser for macOS focused on
minimalism, privacy, and calm design.

   Is it free and open‑source?

Yes. Nook is open‑source and free to use. You can explore and
support the project on GitHub and Open Collective.

   Do you sell my browsing data?

No. We do not sell your browsing data. Our pledge is privacy first,
with settings that are understandable and reversible.

   Does Nook support Chrome extensions?

Yes. Nook supports Chrome extensions so you can keep your workflow
familiar.

   Is AI required to use Nook?

No. AI features are strictly opt‑in. The browser is fast and fully
usable without them.

   Where can I download Nook?

Visit the [download](/download) page to get the latest build for macOS.
